Two apps, one padel platform

Everything you need, on and off the court

Padeja is built from two connected apps that share your profile and match data.

🎾

Padeja Social

Your padel network & stats

The social side of padel. Follow friends, watch their live matches, keep every result, and see where you rank.

  • ✓ A live feed of your friends' matches as they happen
  • ✓ Detailed stats — win rate, streaks, partnerships & form
  • ✓ Browse & join public leagues and competitions near you
  • ✓ Monthly leaderboards for your club and friends
  • ✓ A marketplace to buy and sell rackets in your network
🍎 App Store ▶ Google Play
⚡

Padeja Live

Real-time match scoring

The scoring engine. Track every point live, share the score to your friends' feeds, and host your own leagues and competitions.

  • ✓ Point-by-point live scoring, built for padel
  • ✓ Score from your Apple Watch with a paired phone
  • ✓ Live-share scores straight to the Social feed
  • ✓ Host leagues, Americano & Mexicano competitions
  • ✓ Rich game-by-game data powers your Social stats
🍎 App Store ▶ Google Play
