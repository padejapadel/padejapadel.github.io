Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

This privacy policy explains what information Padeja Feed collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. Padeja Feed is a social app for padel players, allowing you to follow friends, share match updates, message other users, and buy and sell padel equipment within your network.

Information we collect

We collect the following information when you use Padeja Feed:

  • Account information: your email address, used to create and sign in to your account.
  • Profile information: your display name, location (as a text field you enter, e.g. "Essex"), padel level, and profile photo.
  • Content you create: posts, comments, likes and reactions, racket listings (including photos and prices), and direct messages you send to other users.
  • Match data: if you use Padeja Feed alongside Padeja Live, match results, scores, and live match updates are shared between the two apps using the same account.
  • Social connections: who you follow and who follows you within the app.
  • Push notification token: a device identifier used to deliver notifications (e.g. new followers, messages, comments) to your phone. This token is specific to your device and the Padeja Feed app.
  • Photos: any photos you choose to upload — for your profile picture, racket listings, or feed posts.

Information we don't collect

Padeja Feed does not access your contacts, browsing history, or any data from other apps on your device. We do not track your precise location in the background — location is only used if you actively choose to share it as part of a feature within the app, and is never collected automatically.

How we use your information

  • To provide the core features of the app — your feed, friends list, messaging, and marketplace
  • To send push notifications for activity relevant to you (new followers, comments, likes, messages, live matches from friends)
  • To display your profile and content to other users you connect with, as intended by the app's social features
  • To maintain the security and reliability of the service

Who can see your information

Your profile, posts, and racket listings are visible to other Padeja Feed users according to the app's normal social features — for example, your posts are visible to people who follow you, and marketplace listings are visible to your followers. Direct messages are only visible to you and the person you're messaging. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database and backend provider. Photos are stored using Supabase Storage. Push notifications are delivered via Apple Push Notification service (iOS) or Firebase Cloud Messaging (Android).

Your choices

You can:

  • Edit or delete your profile information at any time within the app
  • Delete individual posts, comments, or racket listings you've created
  • Unfollow or block other users to control who sees your content
  • Request deletion of your entire account and associated data — see our account deletion page for instructions

Children's privacy

Padeja Feed is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at support@padeja.com.
