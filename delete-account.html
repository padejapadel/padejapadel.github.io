Delete Your Account

If you'd like to delete your Padeja Feed account and associated data, please email us using the address below. We'll process your request and confirm once it's complete.

support@padeja.com Tap to email us

Please send this request from the email address associated with your Padeja Feed account, so we can verify it's you. Include your display name in the app if you can, to help us find your account faster.

What gets deleted
  • Your profile information (name, location, level, avatar)
  • Your posts, comments, and likes on the feed
  • Your racket inventory and marketplace listings
  • Your direct messages and conversations
  • Your follows and followers relationships
  • Your saved leagues and tournament data
Timeframe

We aim to process deletion requests within 14 days of receiving your email. You'll receive a confirmation once your data has been removed.

Note: if you also use Padeja Scorer with the same account, this request only covers Padeja Feed data. Let us know in your email if you'd like your Padeja Scorer data deleted too.
